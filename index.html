About

Texun Trailboss

Russell H. Ritchey. Independent researcher, poet, and author. Waco, Texas. B.S.M.E., University of Texas at Austin.

Operating entirely outside institutional frameworks. No affiliation with any university, church, or organization. Church of One — outside institutional Christianity, not against it.

Poetry

The Poems

Published at AllPoetry as Texun Trailboss. The anthology Genesis of EDT spans three movements and an appendix.

From The Descent
The question arrives before the answer has a name.
Something is wrong and the wrongness is not yours alone.
You did not invent the weight of it.
You arrived already carrying.
From The Witness
The machines ran the argument down
and hit the same wall every time.
Not religion. Not comfort.
The shape of the space
where only one thing fits.
