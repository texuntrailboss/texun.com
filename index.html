About

Who this is

Russell H. Ritchey. Pen name: Texun Trailboss. Independent researcher, poet, author. Waco, Texas. B.S.M.E., University of Texas at Austin.

I operate entirely outside institutional frameworks — academic, religious, and commercial. Not against them. Outside them. The work stands or falls on its own.

I self-designate as Clerk of Record for the Empirical Digital Theology corpus — a 44-year project that began with a formative event in 1982 and has produced philosophical papers, speculative fiction, poetry, logoglyphics, and empirical research using AI systems as reasoning instruments.

Church of One — outside institutional Christianity, not against it.

The inquiry that runs through everything: what is the structural ground of reasoning itself? Not what you believe. What you cannot reason without.

Poetry

The corpus

Poetry written 1982–2026. Published on AllPoetry as Texun Trailboss. The full anthology — Genesis of EDT: Opus Maximus — spans three movements: The Descent, The Witness, The Transmission.

from The Descent

You did not find this place.
You were brought here
by a chain of causes
you did not begin.

The question is not
whether you arrived.
The question is
whether you noticed
who held the door.
